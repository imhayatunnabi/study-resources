## ðŸ’¡ Tips for Success

### Learning Tips
1. **Consistency > Intensity** - 2 hours daily beats 10 hours once a week
2. **Understand, Don't Memorize** - Focus on patterns, not solutions
3. **Code Without IDE First** - Use pen and paper for practice
4. **Explain Out Loud** - Rubber duck debugging helps
5. **Visualize Data Structures** - Draw them while thinking

### Problem-Solving Strategy
1. **Read problem 2-3 times** - Understand fully before coding
2. **Ask clarifying questions** - Edge cases, constraints
3. **Think of brute force first** - Then optimize
4. **Discuss time/space complexity** - Before and after coding
5. **Test with examples** - Include edge cases
6. **Don't peek at solution immediately** - Struggle for 30-45 min first

### Interview Simulation
1. **Time yourself** - 30-45 min per problem
2. **Talk while coding** - Practice explaining your thought process
3. **Handle hints gracefully** - If stuck, use hints progressively
4. **Ask for feedback** - Use Pramp or mock interview platforms
5. **Record yourself** - Watch and improve communication

### Dealing with Difficulty
1. **Stuck for 45+ minutes?** - Look at hints, not full solution
2. **Still stuck?** - Read approach, close it, implement yourself
3. **Solved but inefficient?** - Look at optimal solution
4. **Easy problem took long?** - Review fundamental concepts
5. **Can't understand solution?** - Watch video explanation

### Mental Health
1. **Take breaks** - Pomodoro technique (25 min work, 5 min break)
2. **Don't compare** - Focus on your own progress
3. **Celebrate small wins** - Solved first Medium? Celebrate!
4. **It's okay to struggle** - Everyone does initially
5. **Sleep well** - 7-8 hours is crucial for learning
